Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2024

At inframe, we take privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We are committed to transparency and giving you control over your data.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data through our desktop application and web dashboard:

  • Presence Data: Whether an employee is detected at their workstation (present/away). This is determined using local AI processing on the employee's device. No video or images from the webcam are ever transmitted or stored.
  • Screenshots: Periodic screenshots of the employee's screen (configurable by the employer). These are transmitted securely to the employer's dashboard.
  • App Usage Data: Which applications are in the foreground and for how long. This helps employers understand productivity patterns.
  • Account Information: Email addresses and names for supervisor and employee accounts.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Employer Analytics: Providing real-time and historical insights to authorized supervisors about employee presence and productivity.
  • Service Improvement: Aggregated, anonymized data may be used to improve our algorithms and service quality.
  • Communication: Sending important service updates and notifications.

We never sell your data to third parties. Your data belongs to you and your employer, not us.

3. Data Loss Prevention (DLP)

For Pro and Enterprise customers, our DLP feature automatically detects sensitive windows (banking sites, password managers, personal email, etc.) and blurs them in screenshots before transmission. This ensures employee privacy while still providing work visibility.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Cloud Storage: Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase/PostgreSQL) with regular backups.
  • Access Control: Only authorized supervisors within your company can view employee data. Our support team does not access your data unless required for troubleshooting with your explicit permission.
  • Retention: Data retention periods depend on your plan (7 days for Free, 30 days for Pro, customizable for Enterprise). Older data is automatically deleted.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and contractual obligations).
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@inframe.io.

6. Cookies

Our web dashboard uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Terms of Service

By using inframe, you agree to our Terms of Service. Key points include:

  • You must have legal authority to monitor employees in your jurisdiction.
  • You are responsible for informing your employees that monitoring software is in use.
  • You agree not to use inframe for illegal purposes.
  • We reserve the right to suspend accounts that violate these terms.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on our website.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@inframe.io
  • Support: support@inframe.io
