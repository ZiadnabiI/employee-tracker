Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Pro features for free?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of Pro with all features. No credit card required to start.

How does billing work?

Pro is billed per active user per month. You only pay for users who have the desktop app installed and active.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry best practices for security. See our Privacy Policy for details.
