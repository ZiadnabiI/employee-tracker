Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really a free plan?

The Basic plan is just $5 per user/month. Perfect for small teams to try inframe.

How does annual billing work?

Pay for 10 months upfront and get 12 months of access (2 months free!). This helps you save money and helps us plan for infrastructure costs.

What counts as an "active user"?

You only pay for employees who have the desktop app installed and actively used during the billing period. Inactive users are not charged.

Why no screenshots in Basic?

Screenshots require significant storage space. By excluding them from Basic, we can offer the free tier sustainably. Upgrade to Pro for smart screenshots with privacy blur.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Absolutely! Upgrade instantly and get access to new features right away. Downgrade anytime before your next billing cycle with no penalties.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry best practices for security. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
